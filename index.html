DATA SOURCES
Position & Velocity
Real-time state vectors from JPL Horizons API (spacecraft ID -1024). NASA's authoritative ephemeris system, updated continuously during the mission.
Verify yourself: ssd.jpl.nasa.gov/horizons → Target: "Artemis II" → Ephemeris Type: Vectors
Distance from Earth
Computed as the magnitude of the geocentric position vector (sqrt of X²+Y²+Z²), converted from km to miles.
Distance to Moon
Derived from Earth-Moon mean distance (238,900 mi) minus distance from Earth. For exact selenocentric distance, check Horizons with Center = Moon (500@301).
Mission Elapsed Time
Computed locally from launch time (Apr 1, 2026 17:00 UTC). Cross-reference with NASA AROW.
News Feed
RSS from NASA.gov, Space.com, and Spaceflight Now, filtered for Artemis/lunar keywords. Supplemented with curated X (Twitter) posts from crew and NASA accounts.
Refresh Interval
Telemetry refreshes every 2 minutes. News refreshes every 5 minutes. MET updates every frame.
Fallback: if JPL Horizons is unreachable, telemetry switches to a physics simulation (indicated by a red status dot, bottom-left).
